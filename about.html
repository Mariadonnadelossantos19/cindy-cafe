ABOUT CINDY'S CAFE

Owner of Cindy's Cafe

Cindy's Cafe

Welcome to Cindy's Cafe, a culinary oasis nestled in the heart of Malusak and Tabi Boac, where delightful flavors and warm hospitality converge to create a truly exceptional dining experience. At Cindy's Cafe, we take pride in crafting a diverse and mouthwatering menu that caters to every palate. Our chefs are dedicated to delivering an array of delectable dishes, from juicy, gourmet burgers that redefine the art of comfort food, to savory and satisfying pasta creations that transport you to the heart of Italy. Indulge your taste buds with our meticulously crafted menu, designed to tantalize your senses. Each bite is a journey of flavors, meticulously prepared with the freshest ingredients to ensure a dining experience that is nothing short of extraordinary. Quench your thirst with our extensive selection of beverages, ranging from refreshing and innovative drinks to classic favorites. Whether you're in the mood for a zesty fruit smoothie, a rich cup of coffee, or a handcrafted cocktail, we have the perfect beverage to complement your meal. We invite you to savor the moments and treat yourself to an unforgettable dining experience at Cindy's Cafe. Join us in celebrating the joy of good food, good company, and good times. Indulge your senses, and let Cindy's Cafe be your go-to destination for culinary bliss in Malusak and Tabi Boac. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we look forward to welcoming you to a world of flavors at Cindy's Cafe – where every bite tells a story, and every visit feels like coming home.

Mildred M. Osicos

CEO of Cindy's Cafe
★★★★★

Thanks for coming to Cindy's Cafe! Cindy's Cafe is a culinary paradise where home-cooked goodness meets the comfort of a cozy space. Mildred M. Osicos established Cindy's Cafe on November 18, 2022, with a lot of energy. It has since become a popular place for visitors as well as locals to meet. We are in the middle of Boac and can be found in two handy places: Tabi Boac and Malusak Boac. Cindy's Cafe is ready to greet you with open arms whether you're experiencing the lively streets of Tabi Boac or taking it easy in Malusak Boac.  Cindy's Cafe is more than just a place to eat. It's a story of love, desire, and hard work for good food. The happy owner, Mildred M. Osicos, wanted to create a place where family and friends could enjoy tasty meals in a friendly atmosphere. Cindy's Cafe has always been dedicated to offering hearty, home-cooked meals made with love.
